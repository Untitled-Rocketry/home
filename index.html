UNTITLED ROCKETRY

Mission Statement

We are Untitled Rocketry, a high school team competing in the American Rocketry Challenge, building 3D printed rockets, advancing in high power rocketry, and pioneering liquid rocket development. We have been national finalists for 2 years running, dedicated to pushing the boundaries of student rocketry with precision, creativity, and innovation.

Projects

3D Printed Rocket Series
Building and testing fully 3D printed airframes for optimized weight and strength.
Liquid Rocket Development
Designing and testing liquid propulsion systems for high-altitude and experimental rocketry.
American Rocketry Challenge
Competing with thousands of teams across the country for accuracy and precision.
Level 1 Certification
Building and launching the most powerful rockets we can.
